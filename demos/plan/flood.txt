                <span class="section-label">The Solution</span>
                <h2>Physics Meets Vision: Multi-Modal Intelligence</h2>
                <p class="intro-text fade-in">We don't just train a model to recognize water. We teach it to <strong>understand how water behaves</strong>‚Äîcombining radar that sees through clouds, optical data that reveals color, and physical laws that govern flow.</p>
                <div class="methodology-flow">
                    <div class="method-step fade-in">
                        <div class="step-number">01</div>
                        <h3>Sentinel-1 SAR</h3>
                        <p><strong>Radar sees what eyes cannot.</strong> Synthetic Aperture Radar penetrates clouds, fog, darkness‚Äîmeasuring backscatter where water creates mirror-smooth surfaces (VV polarization < 0.2). Day or night, storm or sun, we see everything.</p>
                        <div class="tech-tags">
                            <span class="tech-tag">Cloud-Penetrating</span>
                            <span class="tech-tag">Day/Night</span>
                        </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="method-arrow">+</div>
                    <div class="method-step fade-in">
                        <div class="step-number">02</div>
                        <h3>Sentinel-2 Optical</h3>
                        <p><strong>Color reveals context.</strong> Near-infrared bands compute NDWI (water index), distinguish sediment from clear water, separate seasonal wetlands from catastrophic inundation. Nine spectral bands create a fingerprint only water can match.</p>
                        <div class="tech-tags">
                            <span class="tech-tag">Multispectral</span>
                            <span class="tech-tag">10m Resolution</span>
                        </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="method-arrow">+</div>
                    <div class="method-step fade-in">
                        <div class="step-number">03</div>
                        <h3>Physics-Informed AI</h3>
                        <p><strong>Laws of nature constrain predictions.</strong> We embed Saint-Venant shallow water equations directly into the neural network. Water can't violate physics‚Äîit flows downhill, conserves mass, follows momentum. Our model learns patterns <em>and</em> obeys reality.</p>
                        <div class="tech-tags">
                            <span class="tech-tag">Differential Equations</span>
                            <span class="tech-tag">Conservation Laws</span>
                        </div>
                    </div>
                </div>
                <div class="breakthrough-callout fade-in">
                    <div class="breakthrough-icon">üî¨</div>
                    <div class="breakthrough-content">
                        <h4>The Scientific Breakthrough</h4>
                        <p>Traditional models pattern-match. Ours <strong>simulates</strong>. By fusing temporal transformers (attention across time) with physics-informed layers (fluid dynamics constraints), we don't just detect floods‚Äîwe understand their mechanics. Result: <strong>96-99% accuracy</strong> even on unprecedented flood geometries never seen during training.</p>
